## Quick Summary

**Catholic Ride Share** is a free, volunteer-based ride-sharing platform built to ensure that no Catholic is prevented from attending Mass, Confession, or other parish activities simply because they lack transportation. It was created by [Catholic.Work](Catholic-Work), a nonprofit led by [Barry Schoedel](Barry-Schoedel), and is open-source and community-driven.

- 🚗 **Riders** request a lift to Mass, Confession, or parish events.
- 🙏 **Volunteer drivers** choose which rides they are willing to take.
- 💰 **No payment required** – optional post-ride donations only.
- 🌾 **Rural-friendly** – no hard distance limit; distance is information, not a gate.
- 🔒 **Privacy-first** – minimal data collection; locations blurred until a ride is accepted.
